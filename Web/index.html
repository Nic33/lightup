lightup

The aim of the game is to light up all the blank squares. To do this, you have to place lightbulbs on various squares in the grid. However not all squares can have lightbulbs. And some squares require a certain number of lightbulbs touching them. The Darth Vader squares can touch any number of lightbulbs. Click a square once to place a lightbulb. Click it twice to place a marker, so that you know a lightbulb will not go there. Click a third time and to remove the marker, thus making it blank again. On the bottom of the screen there are buttons for help, quit, redo a move, undo a move, save the game and restart the game. If you find yourself unable to solve the game, just click on "Solve", he'll know how to solve it.
